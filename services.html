    Services

    Data Annotation

    • Image Annotation: Our image annotation service enables you to leverage the potential of computer vision technology. We meticulously annotate images by adding bounding boxes, polygons, points, or semantic segmentation masks to precisely identify and outline objects or regions of interest within the images. Whether you need object detection, object recognition, or image segmentation, our image annotation service ensures that your models are trained on the right data.
    • Video Annotation: Videos hold a wealth of valuable information, and our video annotation service ensures that this data is fully utilized for your AI projects. We offer frame-by-frame annotation, allowing you to track and annotate objects or regions of interest across the video. With precise bounding boxes or polygons, your models can excel in tasks like object tracking, action recognition, and behavior analysis.
    • Lidar Annotation: Working with 3D point cloud data from Lidar sensors requires accurate and detailed annotations. Our Lidar annotation service covers various aspects, from classifying objects to capturing their sizes, shapes, and orientations. With our expertise in annotating 3D point cloud data, you can develop advanced AI solutions for autonomous vehicles, robotics, and 3D environment understanding.
    • Audio/Speech Annotation: Our audio/speech annotation service is tailored to unlock the potential of sound data. Whether it's transcribing speech into text for automatic speech recognition (ASR) models or tagging specific sound events within audio recordings, our detailed annotations ensure that your models can process and comprehend audio data effectively. This service is ideal for voice assistants, audio analysis, and speech-related applications.

    Natural Language Processing

    • Named Entity Recognition (NER) ; Our NER service enables automatic identification and classification of entities within textual data, such as names of people, organizations, locations, dates, and more. By extracting key entities from unstructured text, businesses can gain valuable insights, improve search algorithms, and enhance information retrieval systems.
    • Text Analysis: With our advanced text analysis capabilities, we delve deep into textual content to derive meaningful patterns and trends. Our text analysis service involves language detection, part-of-speech tagging, and syntactic parsing, enabling a deeper understanding of the structure and context of text data.
    • Sentiment Analysis: Harness the sentiment behind your textual data with our sentiment analysis service. We utilize powerful machine learning algorithms to determine the sentiment (positive, negative, neutral) expressed in text. This service is invaluable for gauging customer feedback, monitoring brand reputation, and making data-driven decisions.
    • Text Classification: Delegate Operations excels in text classification, categorizing textual content into predefined classes or categories. Whether it's classifying support tickets, news articles, or customer reviews, our service enables efficient content organization and streamlines information retrieval.
    • Text Summarization: Our text summarization service condenses lengthy text into concise and coherent summaries while retaining essential information. Ideal for handling large volumes of text, this service saves time and allows users to quickly grasp the main points from lengthy documents.
    • Text Completion and Auto-correction: Delegate Operations offers intelligent text completion and auto-correction services, optimizing user experience and ensuring error-free communication. Our technology automatically suggests completions for partially typed text and fixes spelling and grammatical errors in real-time.

    Vision AI

    • Object Detection and Recognition: Our Vision AI solutions excel in detecting and recognizing objects within images and videos. By leveraging state-of-the-art deep learning models, we enable businesses to automatically identify and categorize objects, facilitating applications like inventory management, security surveillance, and autonomous systems.
    • Image Classification: Delegate Operations empowers businesses to classify images into predefined categories or classes. Whether it's identifying different product types, classifying medical images, or analyzing satellite imagery, our image classification service helps businesses organize and process vast amounts of visual data.
    • Image Segmentation: With our advanced image segmentation capabilities, we accurately separate objects and regions within images. This service is instrumental in applications like medical image analysis, autonomous driving, and understanding complex visual scenes.
    • Optical Character Recognition (OCR): Delegate Operations offers robust OCR services to extract text from images and convert it into editable and searchable formats. This enables businesses to digitize documents, automate data entry, and enhance information retrieval from scanned materials.
    • Visual Search: Our Vision AI solutions power visual search capabilities, allowing users to find products or information by using images as queries. This technology enhances e-commerce experiences, simplifies image-based searches, and fosters seamless customer engagement.
    • Facial Recognition and Analysis: Leveraging advanced facial recognition algorithms, Delegate Operations enables accurate identification and analysis of human faces. Our facial recognition services find applications in security and surveillance, access control, and personalized user experiences.
